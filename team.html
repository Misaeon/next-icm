Sen Miao

Sen Miao is the principal investigator of the project. He is currently a postdoc research fellow at the Division of Building Services Engineering, Chalmers University of Technology.

His research focuses on Building Informatics and Indoor Environmental Quality, with particular interests in:

  • Analyzing key factors influencing indoor environmental quality and occupants' perceptions
  • Modeling human–building interactions under system uncertainties
  • Developing advanced predictive models and control algorithms for intelligent building management

Lars Ekberg

Lars Ekberg is the host supervisor of the project. He is a Professor in indoor climate technology at the Division of Building Services Engineering, Chalmers University of Technology. He also works as a consultant at one of the subsidiaries of Chalmers Industriteknik, CIT Energy Management AB.

Lars' research is focused on the chain of issues from human needs and wishes, via formulation of requirements to the design, construction and management of healthy, comfortable and energy-efficient buildings. He works over a wide spectrum, with residential buildings on one end, and advanced laboratories with very stringent requirements on the other.

Despoina Teli

Despoina Teli is the project advisor. She is an associate professor at the Division of Building Services Engineering, Chalmers University of Technology. She is also profile leader of 'Energy in Urban Development' of the Chalmers Energy Area of Advance.

Despoina’s research focuses on indoor climate, with a particular interest in thermal environment, human comfort and the interaction between buildings and their occupants.
